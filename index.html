About Mecharena

MECHARENA is an annual inter-college National level technical festival organised by the students of the Department Of Mechanical Engineering. It was launched in 2002 with the motive of encouraging scientific temper, fostering innovation and improving managerial skills among student fraternity and providing them a platform to enhance their skills. It hosts a plethora of competitions, workshops, lectures and paper presentations. It has evolved and grown, fulfilling its purpose and becoming one of the most sought after student technical fest in the country. In it's 19th edition, Mecharena is back with a bang, conducting events catering to every imaginable field of Mechanical Engineering ranging from 3D printing, IC-Engines to Robotics along with attractive prizes to be won. State of the art experts lectures and workshops from diverse spheres of science and technology are organised to update the knowledge and skill of participants.
